How to take your bakery to the next level?

Por A&A Baking 0 Comentarios 7 Octubre, 2020

In times of crisis, innovation offers to companies the opportunity to adjust and evolve. Due to the pandemic and the space restrictions in production areas, where six people used to operate, now, spacing protocols only allows three to work. This, applied to manual production processes, represents a great productivity challenge affected by the health priority.

A&A Baking Solutions has a set of solutions that allow to keep up the productivity and meet regulations. With automated production lines of only 65m2, to produce up to 2.000 pieces of bread per hour with only three qualified operators.

If you ask yourself what to do with the additional personnel who cannot work because of biosecurity protocols, the answer is to create, create and create, new recipes, products and strategies. The challenge is to talk to the end consumer and ask them what they would like to taste; to investigate what types of bread they would like to have to satisfy their demand and eating experience. In addition, offer types of bread suitable for lunch, dinner and snacks, developed with recipes of complementary ingredients (inserts) such as seeds, nuts, chocolate, alternative flours to wheat.

With an automated production system, you can expand your portfolio and consider expanding to more areas of the city, you can reach new types of customers and if you add a digital positioning strategy and promotion, your business can reach the next level sooner than you think.
