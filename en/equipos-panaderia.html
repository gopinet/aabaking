MACHIENARY

Porlanmaz Bakery Machinery Co is our ally in bakery technology, with whom after several years of teamwork we have carried out high-impact revolutionary projects with great standards of excellence.
It is one of the leading manufacturers of baking equipment since 2001 in Turkey. The high standards of technology and European quality make them the perfect ally for the development of your business.

  • Dough Mixer

    PMSP60

    • It enables to make very little flour to dough.
    • It also provides more homogenate dough mixture and bigger volume bread.
    • The machine works in manual and automatic cycle with two programmable timers.
    • The bowl, spiral arm and bar are made of stainless steel.
    • Reverse bowl rotation.
    • Due to belt transmissions of main power the machine noise level is quite low.
    • The mixer is mobile and there is a fixing device on the mixer.
    • The emergency stop function and the security system on the cover are available.
    • It can be moved easily thanks to its wheels.

    Capacity of dough: 60 kg

    Also available for capacity:
    120 kg / 160 kg / 250 kg

  • Dough Divider

    PMVD2000

    • Cuts the dough at the requested weight without pressing and harming the dough.
    • All the surfaces contacting the dough are made of stainless steel.
    • It cuts the dough with its weight due to the vacuum generated by piston cylinder movement.
    • All surfaces are lubricated with the oil which is compatible of food.
    • The hopper is made of stainless steel.

    Capacity of 60 to 150 kg of dough

    Cuts the dough in three ranges:
    50 gr - 200 gr
    100 gr - 600 gr
    200 gr - 1000 gr

  • Conical Rounder

    PMCR2000

    • The conical rounder machine shapes the dough thanks to a cone made of aluminum.
    • It has aluminum sleeves around the cone, which make the dough move spirally.
    • The dough is perfectly rounded while moving from bottom to top in the tracks.
    • It is equipped with a newly designed mechanical flour duster which does not produce any noise while working.
    • All moving part have bail bearings and the machine is on wheels.
    • Due to the all the aluminum components, it can be coated with teflon as per the request.

  • Proofer

    PMCR2000

    • This machine is specially designed for the cold and hot climate.
    • Dough is circulated at all of the pockets one by one with
    the transferring method in the machine.
    • This circulation stays 8 minutes in the cold climates and 5 minutes in the hot climates.
    • Right and left outlet is provided due to the fact that the transferring mechanism is adjustable.
    • Easy and hygienic cleaning is provided because the conveying nets are plastic and replaceable.
    • It can be moved to any place because it is on wheels.
    • Some parts of the machine are covered by transparent material to observe the dough.
    • Machine is made of stainless steel.

    Canal capacity: 240 pieces
    Capacity: 1.800 – 2.000 pieces per hour
    Weight of the fermented dough: 100 – 1.500 gr
    Time: 8 minutes

  • Dough Moulder

    MPDM400

    • It shapes the dough, which is opened up very flat by the help of two polyamide rollers,
    at any length and thickness by the means of 2 or 4 rollers.
    • The roller can be adjusted between 0-25 mm without any position.
    • There is a spring scraping mechanism made of stainless steel on the roller.
    • The dough can be shaped at 420mm length due to the polyester woven and resistance
    resistance increasıng long-life conveying band
    and adjustable roller group.
    • Is suitable for all dough pieces, including
    tin bread and small baguette.
    • All rollers are equipped with nylon scrapers which are fixed to the frame.
    • The curling chain is made from stainless steel.
    • Pressing board is adjustable and can be removed for cleaning purposes.
    • Capacity: 2.500 pieces per hour.
    • Weight of the moulded dough: 100 – 1.200 gr.
    Pressing boards: 1 | Rollers: 4

    Mold length options:
    400 mm / 600 mm

  • Oven

    PMDF200

    • The oven provides equal heat distribution. It operates at high production and regular circulation.
    • The well-designed heat exchangers ensure more uniform baking with lower energy consumption.
    • Burning chamber is made of heat resistive (more than 1000 centigrade degree) special stainless steel.
      • By means of the plate-tube system, high level steam is produced and each of the pans is adequately provided with the steam.
    • The rotary ovens are produced with side burner, which is suitable long, narrow rooms and
    in extra compact dimensions. • The halogen lamps, which are mounted the on the door, offer an excellent illumination and they are easy to replace.
    • The steam is prevented to be dispersed in the workshop thanks to the hood and aspirator over the oven.
    • Baking chamber, all outside hoods and chimney hood are fully made of stainless steel.
    • It is recommended that a water softening unit is fitted to the water supply of all ovens.

  • Automatic Bread Slicer

    PMBS1500

    • A perfectly designed machine for high scale bakeries, kitchens, restaurants, hotels, and military units.
    • It has wheels that provide mobility to the machine.
    • It does not deform the shape of the bread since there is not pressure and pushing effort.
    • Tapes speeds and the distance between the tapes can automatically be adjusted
    depending on the shape, crust hardness and height of the bread.
    • It has speed control system 
    • All surfaces which are in contact with the bread are made of food compatible material.
    • Works silent and without vibration.
